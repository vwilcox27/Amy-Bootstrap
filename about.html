Small Cell Cervical Cancer

Small Cell Cervical Carcinoma affects only 1% of cervical cancer patients. To this day, it is not recognized by the cervical cancer board. 99% of cervical cancer victims are conflicted with Cervical Squamous Cell Carcinoma. Both types of cancer are vicious and deadly, but this site is dedicated to the 1% (like my wife) that have Small Cell Cervical Carcinoma. There is little study on this form of cancer and that is why this website is here - to build awareness of this other deadly form of cancer.
